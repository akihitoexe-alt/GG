[ Trap / Normal ]

When this card resolves, you can Special Summon it in Defense Position as a Normal Monster (Warrior / DARK / Level 3 / ATK 0 / DEF 0). (This card is not treated as a Trap.)

(1) When this card is activated: "The Phantom Knights" and "Xyz Dragon" monsters you control cannot be destroyed by battle this turn.

(2) If you have a "The Phantom Knights" monster or an "Xyz Dragon" Xyz Monster in your GY: You can banish this card from your GY; this turn, you take no battle damage, and once, if an Xyz Monster you control would be destroyed by battle or card effect, you can detach 1 material from it instead.

You can only use each effect of "The Phantom Knights' Regretful Shield" once per turn.
