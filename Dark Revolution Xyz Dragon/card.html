● If this card is destroyed by an opponent's card effect and sent to the GY: You can Special Summon 1 "The Phantom Knights" or "Xyz Dragon" Xyz Monster from your GY, ignoring its Summoning conditions, and if you do, attach this card to the Special Summoned monster as material.
