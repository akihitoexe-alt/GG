# Dark Renaissance Xyz Dragon

**Card Type:** Monster
**Attribute:** DARK
**Type:** Dragon / Xyz / Effect
**Rank:** 5
**ATK:** 3000
**DEF:** 2500

**Materials:**
3 Level 5 DARK monsters

**Card Effect:**
If this card has "Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon" as Xyz Material, it gains these effects.
● Once per turn: You can detach 1 Xyz Material from this card, then target 1 face-up monster your opponent controls; change its ATK to 0, and if you do, you gain Life Points equal to half that monster's original ATK.
● When your opponent activates a Spell/Trap card or effect (Quick Effect): You can detach 1 Xyz Material from this card; negate the activation, and if you do, destroy that card, then target 1 "The Phantom Knights" or "Xyz Dragon" monster in your GY; Special Summon it to your side of the field or attach it to this card as Xyz Material.

## Design Notes & Lore
- **Name & Theme:** The word "Renaissance" means "rebirth" or "revival". This perfectly aligns with the card's effect to resurrect a fallen DARK Xyz Monster from the Graveyard and attach materials from the Graveyard to sustain itself.
- **Synergy:** Like "Dark Requiem Xyz Dragon", it requires 3 Level 5 DARK monsters naturally, but is meant to be summoned using a Rank-Up-Magic spell like "The Phantom Knights' Rank-Up-Magic Launch" targeting "Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon".
- **Counterpart to Requiem:** While "Dark Requiem Xyz Dragon" is designed to crush monster effects and acts as monster-based disruption, "Dark Renaissance Xyz Dragon" provides spell/trap disruption, acting as a protector against backrow threats. By having both in your Extra Deck, you have the flexibility to Rank-Up into whichever dragon suits your current situation.
