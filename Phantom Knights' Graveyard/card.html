The Phantom Knights' Graveyard

魔法
✙ Spell Card
[ Spell Card  ✙  Field ]

Your opponent cannot target "The Phantom Knights" Xyz Monsters or "Xyz Dragon" Xyz Monsters you control with Spell/Trap cards or effects, also they cannot be destroyed by your opponent's Spell/Trap effects.

The activation of your cards and effects in the GY that mention "The Phantom Knights" cannot be negated.

When this card is activated: You can add 1 "The Phantom Knights" card from your Deck to your hand, or send 1 "The Phantom Knights" card from your Deck to the GY.

You can only use each of the following effects of "The Phantom Knights' Graveyard" once per turn.

● If a "The Phantom Knights" or "Xyz Dragon" monster you control would be destroyed by battle or card effect, you can detach 1 material from an Xyz Monster you control instead, and if you do, send 1 "The Phantom Knights" card from your Deck to the GY.

● Except the turn this card was sent to the GY, when an opponent's monster declares a direct attack: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "The Phantom Knights" or "Xyz Dragon" Xyz Monster in your GY; Special Summon from your Extra Deck, 1 "The Phantom Knights" or "Xyz Dragon" Xyz Monster that is 1 or 2 Ranks higher than that target, by using it as material. (This is treated as an Xyz Summon. Transfer its materials to the Summoned monster.)

You can only activate 1 "The Phantom Knights' Graveyard" per turn.
