If your LP is 1000 or less, you can activate this card from your hand.

You can only use each effect of "The Phantom Knights' Rank-Up Magic Revenge" once per turn.

ARCHETYPE COMBINATION ANALYSIS

This card represents a unique custom addition to the legendary "The Phantom Knights" archetype, bridging trap activation utility with "Rank-Up-Magic" Xyz Summon capabilities. Styled as a Normal Trap, it features a heavy risk-reward dynamic (paying half LP) to resurrect a fallen phantom warrior and rank it up instantly into a powerful Extra Deck boss monster. Its secondary GY banish ability reflects the characteristic graveyard synergy of Phantom Knights cards, granting a massive ATK surge based on accumulated resources. Finally, the "clutch activation from hand" effect rewards dualists playing under dire circumstances, capturing the core anime theme of staging an epic comeback.
