The Phantom Knights' Shattered Spear
TRAP
[ Trap Card / Continuous ]

You can only activate 1 "The Phantom Knights' Shattered Spear" per turn.

When your opponent activates a card or effect that targets a "The Phantom Knights" and/or "Xyz Dragon" card(s) you control: You can send 1 "Phantom Knights" card from your hand or Deck to the GY, or banish 1 "Phantom Knights" card from your GY; negate that effect, and if you do, inflict 500 damage to your opponent. You can use this effect up to twice per turn.

If a "The Phantom Knights" and/or "Xyz Dragon" card(s) you control would be destroyed by battle or card effect, you can banish this card from your GY instead.
