Raiders' Resistance
SPELL
[ Spell Card / Continuous
]

(This card is always treated as a "Phantom Knights", "Xyz Dragon", and "Raidraptor" card.)

Neither player can activate cards or effects in response to this card's activation.

You cannot Special Summon monsters from the Extra Deck, except DARK Xyz Monsters, the turn you activate this card or any of its effects.

When this card is activated: You can send 1 "Phantom Knights" or "Raidraptor" card from your hand or face-up field to the GY; add 1 "Phantom Knights" or "Raidraptor" card (except "Raiders' Resistance"), or 1 "Rank-Up-Magic" Spell, from your Deck to your hand.

If a "The Phantom Knights", "Xyz Dragon", or "Raidraptor" monster is Special Summoned from the Extra Deck (except during the Damage Step): You can target 1 card on the field; negate its effects (if it is face-up), and if you do, destroy it.

During your Main Phase: You can target up to 3 of your "The Phantom Knights", "Xyz Dragon", and/or "Raidraptor" cards that are banished and/or in your GY (except "Raiders' Resistance"); shuffle them into the Deck, then draw 1 card.

During your Main Phase, except the turn this card was sent to the GY: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "The Phantom Knights", "Xyz Dragon", or "Raidraptor" Xyz Monster you control; until the end of this turn, it is unaffected by other card effects.

You can only activate 1 "Raiders' Resistance" per turn. You can only use each effect of "Raiders' Resistance" once per turn.
