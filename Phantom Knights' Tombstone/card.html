Phantom Knights' Tombstone

∞ Spell Card
[ Spell Card  ∞  Continuous ]

When this card is activated: You can target up to 2 of your banished "The Phantom Knights" and/or "Xyz Dragon" cards; return them to the GY.

During the Battle Phase, face-up "The Phantom Knights" and "Xyz Dragon" monsters you control gain 800 ATK/DEF, also if they attack, your opponent cannot activate cards or effects until the end of the Damage Step.

During your Main Phase, except the turn this card was sent to the GY: You can banish this card from your GY, then target up to 3 "The Phantom Knights" and/or "Xyz Dragon" cards in your GY; shuffle them into the Deck.

You can only activate 1 "Phantom Knights' Tombstone" per turn.
