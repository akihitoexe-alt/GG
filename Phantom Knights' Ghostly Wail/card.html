Phantom Knights' Ghostly Wail

[ Spell Card  ⚡  Quick-Play ]

When your opponent activates a card or effect: Send any number of "The Phantom Knights" cards from your hand and/or face-up field to the GY; target that many face-up cards your opponent controls (including the activated card, if it is on the field); negate their effects until the end of this turn, and if you do, banish them when they leave the field.

Except the turn this card was sent to the GY: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 of your banished "The Phantom Knights" cards; return it to the GY.

You can only activate 1 "Phantom Knights' Ghostly Wail" per turn.
