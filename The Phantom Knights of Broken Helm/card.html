The Phantom Knights of Broken Helm
DARK
[ Warrior / Effect ]

You can only use each effect of "The Phantom Knights of Broken Helm" once per turn.

If a "Phantom Knights" card is sent to your GY and/or banished: You can Special Summon this card from your hand, and if you do, it gains 500 ATK.

If this card is Normal or Special Summoned: You can send 1 "Phantom Knights" card from your hand or Deck to the GY, also you cannot Special Summon monsters for the rest of this turn, except DARK monsters. You can banish this card from your GY (Quick Effect); during the End Phase of this turn, add 1 "Phantom Knights" monster from your GY to your hand, or Set 1 "Phantom Knights" Spell/Trap from your Deck or GY to your field.
