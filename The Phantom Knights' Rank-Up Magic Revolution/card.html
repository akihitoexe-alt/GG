The Phantom Knights' Rank-Up-Magic Revolution
SPELL
[ Spell Card / Quick-Play ]

Activate 1 of these effects;
● During your turn: Target 1 DARK Xyz Monster you control; Special Summon from your Extra Deck, 1 "The Phantom Knights" or "Xyz Dragon" Xyz Monster that is 1 Rank higher than that target, by using it as material. (This is treated as an Xyz Summon. Transfer its materials to the Summoned monster.)
● During your opponent's turn: Target 1 face-up Xyz Monster your opponent controls with no Xyz Materials; take control of that target, then Special Summon from your Extra Deck, 1 "The Phantom Knights" or "Xyz Dragon" Xyz Monster that is 1 Rank higher than that monster, by using it as material. (This is treated as an Xyz Summon. Transfer its materials to the Summoned monster.)
Neither player can activate cards or effects in response to this card's activation.

During your Main Phase: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 DARK Xyz Monster you control; attach 1 "The Phantom Knights" monster from your hand or GY to it as material.

You can only use each effect of "The Phantom Knights' Rank-Up-Magic Revolution" once per turn.
